A web app for minimising wastage when cutting linen and making your own canvases.

It's very simple. No AI. Just create any sized canvases and drag them around to get the most out your linen (cotton etc).

Currently it's best on desktop.  I'll see if I can improve it for mobile.

Help is available in the app.

[**Open LINEN_CUT**](https://lympany.art/linen_cut/app/)
